About Crafty Haven Vinyl


Local Handmade Art

Crafty Haven Vinyl is a Frederick based project created by Dina Caple in 2020 during the Covid-19 pandemic. What started as a way to keep busy during uncertain time quickly transition into a great passion for art and creativity.
