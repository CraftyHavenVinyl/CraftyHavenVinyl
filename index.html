About Crafty Haven Vinyl


Local Handmade Art

Crafty Haven Vinyl is a Frederick based project created by Dina Caple in 2020 during the Covid-19 pandemic. What started as a way to keep busy during uncertain time quickly transitioned into a great passion for art and creativity.

I make custom items using different materials/mediums. I use epoxy resin, vinyl, glitter, mica powders, pigment paste, sublimation and many more. Crafting is my ZEN and I love receiving a custom order and making it come to life.
